Our Story

I L-O-V-E eggs. Nothing beats eating a warm scrambled egg and ham sandwich at 2 in the morning as we pulled an all- nighter to finish that last minute assignment in college or waking up to the wonderful smell of pancakes on a Saturday morning after a long Friday night. The one stable ingredient that I must always have in the fridge at home is eggs. I can make food reminiscent of hearty street food to high-end brunch places that require a minimum of two-hour wait in New York City. I want to create a restaurant that welcomes us to eat eggs at any time of the day because it's just that versatile!

At OvaEasy, we only use organic eggs that come from local and cage-free happy chicks.
